About

Imbibe is an annual online literary journal showcasing the creative work of young creative minds. We welcome poetry, fiction, personal essays, travel stories, graphic designs,opinion pieces as well as art and photography.

The goal of our journal is to assemble in each issue, a broad range of voices, perspectives, and life experiences and provide rich and connectable content to our readers



Expression another word for outburst of the soul



Editor Geetanjali Purohit found Imbibe as a creative experiment for young writers. Every issue is a collective effort made possible by the generosity and time of our various editors and content publishers at large.
