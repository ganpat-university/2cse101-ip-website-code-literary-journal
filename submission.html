Submissions


Thanks for your interest in Imbibe Please take a look at our submission guidelines, and fill out the form below. We look forward to reading your work.



What to Send:


Imbibe is published once a year and accepts submissions of poetry, fiction, non-fiction, essays, opinion pieces, travel writing, photography and art on a rolling basis. We are looking for new work that hasn’t been published anywhere else either online or in print.



Fiction


Send us your stories! Flash, short stories, hybrid forms—all in 1000 words or less.



Non-Fiction

We accept memoir, personal essays, travel adventures. One or two pieces per submission please.Should not exceed 1000 words

Poetry


Upto two poems per submission. Haikus, sonnets and other forms included



Art


Any form of art included. Not more than three peices per submission(scanned,jpeg format) other formats are accepted relative to each case.

Please mail your work to: imbibe.theliteraryjournal@gmail.com
